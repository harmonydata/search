The quantitative element, the British Workplace Behaviour Survey, 2007-2008 (BWBS), is the most comprehensive survey of ill-treatment in the workplace so far undertaken in Britain. The achieved sample of 3,494 included a non-white/non-Christian boost. TNS BMRB were contracted to conduct the survey. (The Department for Business, Innovation and Skills later adopted the same methodology for their 2008/09 Fair Treatment at Work Survey (FTWS), held at the UK Data Archive under SN 6382.)
