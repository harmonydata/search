Such agreements typically spell out what is expected of the service user and what the service will provide in return. The aims of the study are:

to assess the extent of the use of contracts with drug users in treatment
to develop a typology of the form and content of these contracts
to examine the perspectives of both drug treatment professionals and drug users on the nature, purposes, functioning and impact of these contracts
to situate the contractual governance of drug users in the wider context of contemporary strategies of control.

a survey of the use of contracts in treatment settings across three regions in England;
