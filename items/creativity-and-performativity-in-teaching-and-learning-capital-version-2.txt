The merging of the two policies was not pervasive across schools or within schools but there were some examples involving cross-curricular projects. More prominent was a 'bolt on' activity of creative teaching and learning such as special creative curriculum weeks or days. Teaching creatively was the preferred form over teaching for creativity.
