This project aimed to investigate EU conditionality and evaluate its impact on institution-building in states undergoing post-communist transformation in Eastern Europe.
The following were employed as case studies: (i) regional policy and the process of regionalisation and (ii) minority issues. The research was developed around two key and innovative elements.
