This study proposed to conduct a comparative investigation, through in-depth life story interviews, of family and cultural sources for entrepreneurial values and activity in Britain and Russia. In the event, no British interviews were carried out, and the Russian interviews were only partially completed. Interviews were carried out with two members of different adult generations in twelve families from Moscow and ten families from Leningrad. A total of 45 interviews were conducted, which cover 114 audio cassettes.
