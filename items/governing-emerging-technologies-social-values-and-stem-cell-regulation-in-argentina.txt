These interviews were translated and transcribed. 

The interviews, probed the respondentsâ€™ understanding of the scientific and regulatory situation in Argentina, queried their ambitions for regulation (ie: what they felt regulation of regenerative medicine should address and how regulation might be structured), and questioned what values they thought should govern the field and its regulation, including the source of core values.
