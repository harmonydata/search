16:T9bd,Using an extensive programme of monitoring, quantitative research and qualitative studies, this research aimed to evaluate the effectiveness of Job Seeker's Allowance, which replaced Unemployment Benefit and Income Support for unemployed people on 7 October 1996. One sample was drawn before the implementation of Job Seeker's Allowance, the other afterwards.
