This project looked at the 'secret garden' of conservatoires of music. This is an area of higher education where the one-to-one lesson format has traditionally formed the core of teaching and learning. Conservatoires have hitherto been under-researched. The project addressed this gap, working towards a new understanding of how western classical musicians are created and how their learning experiences and outcomes can be enhanced.

This collection contains longitudinal data from September 2004 until July 2007, drawing on interviews, questionnaires, observation and routine institutional assessments.
