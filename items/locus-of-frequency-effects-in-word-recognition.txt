Models of word recognition are influenced heavily by experimental research, and one of the most robust findings is that frequently used words (eg "house") are recognised faster than rare words (eg "larch"). However there is debate over the exact nature of this 'frequency effect', particularly whether it reflects the earliest stages of word perception.
