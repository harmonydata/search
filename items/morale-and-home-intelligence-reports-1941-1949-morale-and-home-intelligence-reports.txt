The Morale Committee for the Allied Forces Headquarters (AFHQ) sent out a directive in 1940 to (AFHQ) requesting quarterly morale reports. The goals of the reports were to study morale of soldiers in its broadest aspect and to coordinate and prepare quarterly morale reports for the War Office.
