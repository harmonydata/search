Interviews were designed using Cognitive Task Analysis (Crandall, Klein & Hoffman, 2006), where participants were interviewed using the Critical Decision Method; a narrative-based approach to understand how experts navigate their complex work domains and has been previously used successfully to understand complex choices in emergency response contexts (Power & Alison, 2017).
