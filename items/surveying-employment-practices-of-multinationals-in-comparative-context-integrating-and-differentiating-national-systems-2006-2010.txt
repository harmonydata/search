The survey aimed to collect data on employment practices for three employee groups: managers, 'key group', and the 'largest occupational group'.

Each documents the variety of employment practices among MNCs and explores the level at which decisions on such issues are determined, how practices are transferred across borders, and how policies are monitored and enforced.

The later research extended this in an exciting and novel direction.
