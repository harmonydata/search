    • TRACK-COVID Study

    TRACK-COVID recruited approximately 20,000 people to determine the risk factors for infection of SARS-CoV-2 and to understand why only some people have COVID-19 symptoms. Participants were recruited from the INTERVAL, COMPARE and STRIDES studies that are part of the National Institute for Health and Care Research (NIHR) Blood Donors Studies BioResource.
